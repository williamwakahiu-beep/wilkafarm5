Avocado Farming Tips

1. Land Preparation

Dig holes 2ft x 2ft x 2ft. Mix topsoil with 1 debe of manure. Space trees 6m x 6m apart. Prepare land 2-3 weeks before rains. Ensure good drainage.

2. Planting

Plant at onset of long rains. Remove polythene bag carefully without breaking soil ball. Graft union should be 15cm above ground. Water immediately after planting.

3. Canopy Management

Prune to open center for sunlight. Remove suckers and dead branches yearly. Keep tree height at 4-5m for easy harvesting. Shape tree when young.

4. Pest & Disease Control

Common pests: Thrips, mites, fruit flies. Spray with recommended insecticide.
Diseases: Root rot, anthracnose. Ensure drainage, use copper-based fungicide.
Scout farm weekly for early detection.

5. Post Harvest Losses Management

Harvest when fruit detaches easily. Handle gently to avoid bruises. Use clean crates, not sacks. Sort by size/quality. Store in cool place. Sell to brokers within 3 days for best price.
